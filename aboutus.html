About us

Who is DCC?

Established in Dubai in 1962, by the late Hasan Abdallah Yabroudi, DCC has grown from a trusted contracting company to a unique, full-service design firm whose curation and expertise deliver unparalleled design ingenuity. Our all-inclusive solutions, and expertise to pick from a network of world-class names in architecture and engineering, ensure that our clients' projects meet the highest standards of quality and sustainability.

What began as a modest family enterprise six decades ago has since evolved into a highly respected enterprise. There’s no limit to what DCC can achieve.
